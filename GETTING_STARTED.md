## 🌐 Accessing Google Earth

### Option 1: Online (Easiest)
1. Go to [earth.google.com](https://earth.google.com)
2. No download required
3. Works in any web browser

### Option 2: Desktop App
1. Download Google Earth Pro for free
2. Works offline
3. Better for large explorations

### Option 3: Mobile App
1. Download Google Earth app from App Store or Google Play
2. Explore on your phone or tablet

## 📱 What You'll Learn

In this course, you will:

✅ **Understand Spectacular Wonders**
- What makes something a "wonder"?
- Natural vs. man-made wonders
- Why they're important to humanity

✅ **Develop Writing Skills**
- Descriptive writing techniques
- How to write about places and monuments
- Vocabulary expansion

✅ **Explore the World**
- Virtual field trips using Google Earth
- Learn about real locations
- Understand geography and culture

✅ **Think Critically**
- Why do we preserve wonders?
- How do natural wonders form?
- What makes something culturally significant?
